Gugk Daeno

Gugk Daeno is the Strongest Phase of 5 steps of anger by Noh Gu, who is an eldest family member of Noh family. As he is known as bad tempered person, in this phase, he will trying to punish people who made him mad.

Gugk Daeno usually happens per decade and some of famly members didn't even experienced it. When it heppens all of family must pack up their important stuff and leave as far as they can. Mostly it takes around 10 to 15 days to loose his anger and they must avoid their appearance from him during those days.
